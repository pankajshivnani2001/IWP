Ingredient FAQs

Ques: Are ingredients fresh or frozen?

All our ingredients are completely fresh and we do not use frozen ingredients. Our ingredients go through six checks and quality is of outmost importance.

Ques: Where can I find allergic information about the products?

All the information about the ingredients is put on a label on the packaging. You can check for any allergic ingredients from that label.

Ques: Do you offer any products with only vegan ingredients?

So far we don't offer any vegan products but we will be soon introducing vegan ingredients in our products

Delivery FAQs

Ques: How long is the delivery time?

We guarantee a delivery time of 30 minutes or less. In case of any late deliveries, you get a full refund, that is, your order is free.

Ques: Is 30 minutes delivery time guaranteed for all products?

Yes, we guarantee a 30-minute delivery time on all our products.

Ques: Do you deliver via Zomato or Swiggy?

We don't deliver by Zomato or Swiggy yet but we plan to bring PizzaAwesome to these platforms soon.

Payment FAQs

Ques: What payment methods are accepted by PizzaAwesome?

You can pay via credit/debit card, UPI, and PayTM wallets on our website. Or you can opt for cash on delivery.

Ques: Can we pay via Bitcoin?

No, we don't accept payments via Bitcoin or any other cryptocurrency but we plan to bring them to PizzaAwesome soon.

Ques: How are refunds carried out?

Any refund will be directly tranferred to your bank account. You need to share your bank details for the refund to take place.

Availability FAQs

Ques: Are all products available the entire day?

So far, we only offer takeouts and delivery. The timings are 10 AM to 11 PM.

Ques: Is PizzaAwesome available in all cities?

  4. Bangalore
  5. Hyderabad

Ques: Is PizzaAwesome open on weekends?

PizzaAwesome is open on all days.
