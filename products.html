Pasta

Pasta is a traditional Italian Dish made from Durum Wheat and Water and stamped into different shapes. We offer seven types of sauces for your Pasta and also offer different shapes. As always, quality is our top priority and we create all the pastas fresh and in-house with top quality ingredients.

  3. Macoroni
  4. Stuffed Pasta
  5. Cannoloni

Risotto

Risotto is also a traditional Italian Dish made from short grain rice called arborio. We prepare our Risotto in a smooth and creamy broth. We use traditional Italian recipes and ingredients for our Risotto. Our Risotto includes Onion, Butter, Parmesan Cheese, and Italian White Wine.

Rice Available
  1. Arborio
  2. Baldo
  3. Carnaroli
  4. Maratelli
  5. Padano
  6. Roma
  7. Vialone Nano

Lasagna

Lasagna originated from the city of Naples, Italy during the middle ages and is considered as one of the best Italian dishes to try. We prepare our Lasagna from flat sheet pasta and it contains four different layers. The toppings and sauces can be customized by you.

Toppings Available
  1. Basil
  2. Parmesan Cheese
  3. Red Pepper Flakes
  4. White or Yellow Onions
Sauces Available
  1. White Cheese Sauce
  2. Creme Sauce
  3. Red Tomato Sauce

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta which is a staple of Italian cuisine. We offer one of the best spaghetti made with natural cylindrical pasta boiled to perfection and cooked with authentic Italian ingredients. We prepare our spaghetti in a traditional red tomata sauce and a mixture of mozerella and parmesan cheese. We offer a side dish of Garlic Bread to go with the spaghetti. You can also choose additional cheese toppings.

Cheese Available
  1. Mozerella
  2. Parmesan
  3. Ricotta
  4. Fontina

Garlic Bread

Garlic Bread is yet another traditional Italian dish that consists of bread(baguette or sour dough) topped with garlic butter and olive oil and mozerella cheese. In addition to the traditional recipe, we offer customizations. The user can choose three additional toppings for the Garlic Bread. Our Garlic Bread is prepared with freshly baked bread and authentic Italian ingredients.

Toppings Available
  1. Onion
  2. Green Pepper
  3. Red Pepper
  4. Chilli Flakes
