Traditional Recipes

PizzaAwesome uses traditional recipes that were developed after a lot of research. We cook our dish in a traditional way with techniques that were developed thousands of years ago by our great-great grandmothers. Our Italian dishes will bring back your childhood memories of Pizza and Pasta created by your grand-mothers. We use traditional mud ovens to cook and bake dishes. Our Pasta and Pizza dough are made in-house and baked fresh.

Original and Fresh Ingredients

PizzaAwesome uses Original and Fresh Ingredients which are imported every morning from farms. We do not use any frozen ingredients. All the dishes are maded from scratch in-house by our expert chefs. Quality is something that we don't compromise on. Our ingredients go through a six-step quality check. Even if a single quality check is failed, we discard all the ingredients and import fresh ones.
